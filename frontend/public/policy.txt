RETURN POLICY

Return Window: 30 days from delivery date
Condition: Items must be unused and in original packaging

How to return:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Orders section
3. Select the order to return
4. Click "Request Return"
5. Print the return label
6. Ship the item back

Refund Processing:
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Original payment method will be credited
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item is defective

Exchanges:
- Available for different sizes/colors
- Same return process applies
- New item ships after return is received
